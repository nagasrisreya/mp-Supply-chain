How to Use This Webpage

  1. Login/Register: If you are a new user, register for an account. If you already have an account, log in to access the features.
  2. Optimize Supply Chain: Click on the "Go to Optimizer" button to input supplier, warehouse, product, and transportation details. Submit the form to calculate the optimal supply chain strategy.
  3. View Inventory: Click on the "View Inventory" button to see the results of your optimization, including details like supplier utilization, warehouse utilization, and total cost.
  4. Manage Inventory: On the inventory page, you can delete individual records or clear all records using the provided buttons.
  5. Logout: When you're done, click the "Logout" button in the navigation bar to securely log out of your account.
